FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS (FAQs)

If you have more questions please contact us and we will reply promptly with more information and details.

IN WHICH LANGUAGES DO YOU OFFER TOURS
Bruno and his team of local guides are Italian but speak fluently other languages with many years of experience. We speak English, French, Spanish and German.
HOW DO I MAKE A RESERVATION?
Contact us via email, phone, online contact form, whatsapp or facebook. we reply promptly with more information about the experience you are interested in and send you payment details. Once you pay, your tours are booked.
WHAT PAYMENT METHOD DO YOU ACCEPT?
You can easily pay online with credit card, stripe or paypal. If it is a last minute booking you can also pay cash just before the tour to our guide. Bank transfers are also accepted.
WHY PRICES ARE NOT SHOWN ON YOUR WEBSITE?
Most of our tours are tailored on you depending on your interests, needs, age of your group etc... We can customize duration of the tour, include more attractions etc... Therefore, prices vary accordingly and there is not one price valid for all. We will give you a quotation as soon as you contact us.
ARE YOUR TOURS ACCESSIBLE TO WHEELCHAIR?
Most of are tours are accessible to wheelchair. However, some attractions may not. Contact us to know more about the experience you are interested in.
DO YOU WORK WITH TRAVEL AGENCIES?
Yes, we partner with travel agencies from all over the world. We provide the expertise and up-date information and services that only locals can master.
Bruno is the founder of a faboulous team of tour consultants, guides and drivers. Almost all of them were born and raised in Italy and that is a guarantee of a truly local experience.
