How To Choose Your Tours

What you would like to see not always matches what you will be able to see.


Guided tours with a local guide give definetely a big plus to your holiday. They make you save time, see more and avoid troubles

But which tours to choose? How many per day? How much free time should I allow? Let's answer to all these questions and more.

  • FIRST THING TO CONSIDER: YOU DO NOT KNOW WHAT IS BEST FOR YOU

    It is like when we were kids and we did not know what to choose or order because of our lack of direct and long experience. We are the expert and we know what is best for you more than you do.

  • WHAT IS THE GOAL OF YOUR VACATION

    People enjoy and have fun in a different way. Moreover some want to learn and explore a lot, others want to relax and take it easy with more dull moments or free time. Having clear in mind what is the goal of your holiday is the best step to start with.

  • CONSIDER YOUR PHISICAL CONDITIONS

    Everybody gets tired sooner or later. You just have to take into consideration what your limits are. Some people love walking others suffer easy fatigue. Just tell us waht your real conditions are and we help you designe the best itinerary for you.

  • PLAN WELL SO YOU DO NOT SEE THINGS TWICE

    There is so much to see that it is best you give each attraction the time it deserves without boring repetitions and overlaps. We will arrange your tours in an efficient way so you will both explore and enjoy withour rushing or losing time.
