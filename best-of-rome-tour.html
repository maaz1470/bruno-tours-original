TOUR DETAILS :

If you are planning your visit to Rome and have limited time, our private tour will give you the great opportunity to explore the most iconic sites of the city in just 1 day. Rome is one of the most beautiful cities in the world with a plenty of monuments worth visiting. Booking our guided tour ensures you'll see the main highlights and that you won't miss anything important.

Enjoy skip-the-line access to the city’s key landmarks, including the Colosseum, Roman Forum and Vatican Museums with Sistine Chapel. ITINERARY :

This is the itinerary of the standard 7 hours tour, but if you wish we can customize it to make it longer or shorter!

  • HOUR 1

    Our tour starts from the Colosseum – the true symbol of Rome and the most famous amphitheatre in the world. It was a place, where the spectacular games and bloody battles of gladiators and exotic animals were held to entertain the Roman people. You will learn all about this ancient monument, its history, traditions and events and also about the complex techniques of construction invented by Romans. Our professional guide with an art and archaeology background will tell you little known facts and fascinating stories about the famous gladiators and their incredible ability.

  • HOUR 2

    Next, we will discover the Roman Forum , one of the most significant archaeological areas in the world. In the past it was the political and commercial centre of the city and the bustling area with important temples, markets and courthouses. You will dig deep into history and learn about the ancient Roman daily life while walking through these impressive ruins, including the Temple of Julius Caesar, Arch of Titus, House of the Vestal Virgins, Senate House and Basilica of Maxentius .

  • HOUR 3

    After visiting the Roman Forum we will reach Piazza Venezia. Located at the base of Capitoline Hill, one of the seven hills of Rome, this large square houses some iconic landmarks, including the enormous monument called “Vittoriano”, dedicated to Vittorio Emanuele II, first king of Italy. The monument is also known as “Altare della Patria” because it contains the altar of the unknown soldier. With your guide you will discover the history of this area and climb up the Capitoline Hill to enjoy the amusing view of the city centre.

  • HOUR 4

    We then take a walk through the city centre to explore its historic treasures. We will see the world-renowned Trevi Fountain, one of the most beautiful baroque monuments in the world and enjoy its gorgeous monumental architecture. Don’t forget to throw the coin in the fountain, because, according to the city’s legend it will ensure your return in Rome. Next, we will continue our sightseeing walk through the picturesque Roman streets where we will see the Renaissance Church of Santi Apostoli, Piazza Colonna with the marble Column of Marcus Aurelius, Piazza di Pietra with the ancient Roman Temple of Hadrian , a great masterpiece of Baroque art – the Church of Sant'Ignazio di Loyola and one of the symbols of Ancient Rome – the splendid Pantheon.

  • HOUR 5

    We will stop here for a lunch break and your guide will suggest a number of excellent places to get a sandwich, a pizza or a three-course meal. After lunch the tour continues with another 2 key attraction of Rome: Piazza Navona and Piazza di Spagna . You will see the famous monuments such as Bernini's Four River Fountain and Borromini's church Santa Agnese in Agone and explore the gorgeous architecture of legendary Spanish Steps.

  • HOUR 6

    After crossing the Tiber River we will reach the highlight of the Tour, the Vatican City. Here we will continue with an exclusive tour of the Vatican Museums and St Peter’s Basilica. Enjoy your skip-the line access and discover the invaluable collection of artworks hosted in the most important museum of Italy. Our guide will show you the significant artworks from Greek and Roman art, Pinacoteca, Raphael’s Rooms, the gallery of statues, the gallery of geographic maps, the gallery of tapestries and much more. Towards the end of this visit you will see the iconic Sistine Chapel decorated with the incomparable Michelangelo’s frescoes.

  • HOUR 7

    Our full-day tour of Rome will finish at the St. Peter's square. We will visit the largest Catholic Church in the world and explore its main art treasures and architecture. Your guide will entertain you with interesting facts and curiosities about the previous Popes and the Basilica.
