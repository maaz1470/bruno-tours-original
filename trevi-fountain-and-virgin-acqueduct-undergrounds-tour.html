The Trevi Fountain in Depth and Virgin Acqueduct Undergrounds Private Tour

THE TREVI FOUNTAIN IN DEPTH AND VIRGIN ACQUEDUCT UNDERGROUDS PRIVATE TOUR

Special guided tour focused on the Trevi Fountain, its Acqueduct and the art wonders that surround it. Learn about history and curiosities of the most famous fountain in the world and explore the underground ruins nearby.

  • 2,5-Hour
  • Private Tour
  • Skip-the-line

TOUR DETAILS :

Visit one most iconic monuments of Rome in depth led by Bruno or one of his top-rated mates. Learn about history and curiosities of the Trevi Fountain and the Acqueduct that supplies the water to it. This ancient Roman Acqueduct still exists and works and we will explore the underground ruins together on this exclusive private guided tour. You will discover the ruins of 2000 years old water pipes and reservoir, decipher the symbols on the Trevi Fountain and other visit other secret corners in the area around it.

It is a private tour, you will have a licensed top-rated local guide only for you and your group. It can be customized to your interests and needs and can be taken any time before the closing time of the attractions. We can arrange transfer from your hotel if you wish. For more info and details please contact us via phone, whatsapp, email, or form. We reply promptly. You will love this tour!
