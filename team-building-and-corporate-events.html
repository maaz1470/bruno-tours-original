Team Building Activities and Corporate Events

WE KEEP CREATING NEW TEAM BUILDING ACTIVITIES AND CORPORATE EVENTS

We are an expert team with a variety of skills and experiences that can help you create the best corporate team building activities and events. From the transfer to the venue, the catering and the conferences: we know how to plan everything down to the smallest detail and give a unique twist to all occasions. With us, you will boost the confidence and the relationships of your team: an essential element for a successful company!

INCENTIVE PROGRAMS AND RETREATS

We plan for you multiple-day incentive trips featuring a vast array of fun activities in the best European cities. Day trips, cooking classes, unique tours: discover the best your location has to offer. And remember, we will always be at your disposal to solve any issue before and during the events.

GUIDED TREASURE HUNTS

Our Treasure Hunts are an original way to explore the greatest landmarks of the city you’re visiting. Participants are divided into teams led by a “hunt master”, and must compete to win the final prize, all while immersing themselves in art, culture and much more.

VIP EXCLUSIVE ACCESS EXPERIENCE

Unique and exclusive experiences for those who want to soak in the atmosphere of the most unique site in Italy. Visit the Sistine Chapel after closing times, or enjoy a fun cocktail party in the Uffizi Gallery: these are only some of the original options we can offer you!

GROUP TASTINGS AND FOOD TOURS

Eat your way through a new city, explore local markets, taste fresh ingredients and sample the best dishes of the traditional cuisine. We offer you a variety of experiences perfect for foodies!

FUN CLASSES AND ON FIELD COURSES

Learn to paint or compose a mosaic; enjoy a private cooking class with an 5 stars chef; take an on-field archaeology lesson with a Phd local expert: a thrilling adventure that will give an original twist to your trip and make it unforgettable!

CONVENTIONS, CONGRESSES; CONFERENCES

We can manage all the details to help you organize your corporate convention or congress. Let our on-site staff take care of the catering, the transfers and everything else you might need.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS (FAQs)

WHERE CAN THE EVENTS AND THE TEAM BUILDING ACTIVITIES TAKE PLACE?
Our events take places in the most important art and business cities in Italy, including (but not limited to) Rome, Venice, Milan, Florence, Naples, Turin.
IN WHICH LANGUAGE ARE THE EVENTS HELD?
Our staff speaks English, Spanish, French, Italian and Chinese.
HOW MUCH DOES EACH EVENT COST?
Our events are customizable, therefore, the final price depends on what you ask us to plan. Some of the variables include: season, number of participants, special requests, amount of staff, etc.
HOW EARLY IN ADVANCE SHOULD YOU CONTACT US TO ORGANIZE YOUR EVENT?
We generally recommend our clients to contact us at least two months in advance.
CAN THE EVENT BE CUSTOMIZED?
Absolutely! We always make sure that the events are tailored on your needs, necessities and company culture. We’re here to help you reach your goals!
DO YOU WORK WITH HUMAN RESOURCES MANAGERs?
Yes, we usually work in constant communication with Hr Managers from all over the world. We provide the expertise and up-date information and services that only locals can master.
