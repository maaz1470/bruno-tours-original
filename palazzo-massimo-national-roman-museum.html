Palazzo Massimo National Roman Museum and Diocletian's Baths Private Tour

PALAZZO MASSIMO NATIONAL ROMAN MUSEUM AND DIOCLETIAN'S BATHS PRIVATE TOUR

Explore the treasures of Ancient Rome, including wall-paintings from the Villa of Empress Livia and visit Diocletian's Baths.

  • 2,5-Hour
  • Private Tour
  • Skip-the-line

TOUR DETAILS :

Housed in beautiful Palazzo Massimo, the National Roman Museum displays one of the richest collections of classical art , including ancient sculptures, magnificent Roman mosaics and original frescoes from the Villa of Empress Livia. Skip long lines with your fast access tickets and head straight inside to explore the museum’s treasures. You will see the highlights such as the “Boxer at Rest” , the “Discus Thrower” , the “Sleeping Hermaphrodite” , the unique collection of ancient Roman calendars and many more. Our expert guide will teach you about art and history and give you insight into the ancient culture and daily lives of the Romans.

Then, we will visit the Diocletian Baths, the largest bath complex in Ancient Rome , commissioned by the Emperor Diocletian in 298 AD. It was twice as large as the famous Baths of Caracalla and could accommodate up to 3000 people at one time. With our guide you will discover Ancient bath system, learn about historic bathing culture in Roman Empire and see how this complex has been transformed into the  Basilica of St. Mary of the Angels  projected by Michelangelo.
